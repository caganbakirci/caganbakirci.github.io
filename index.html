Uncertainty-triggered interventions for VLA policies

Use MC-Dropout entropy as a failure indicator; apply hold-time triggers and offline log-replay to evaluate re-prompt interventions on OCTO.

  • Replaced per-pass probability vectors with per-step entropy (from MC-Dropout) for a cleaner, stabler signal.
  • Built an offline replay “intervention simulator”: apply thresholds/hold-times; report confusion matrices & task-level metrics.
  • OCTO (sim): observed ≈1–2 pp success improvements on some manipulation tasks. Prior RT-1-X (900k, JAX) pilot showed a larger single-task bump but used a non-canonical checkpoint (exploratory only).
